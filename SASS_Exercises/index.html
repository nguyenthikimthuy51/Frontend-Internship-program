About Us

Bakes was established in 2015 by Laure Chevallier, a French hotel entrepreneur, and Tuân Lê, an American creative director of Vietnamese descent.

Our pastry chef is Brian Gartner, from Four Seasons and Ritz-Carlton. The baking team comes from IECD, a French baking academy. Our management team is led by women and is LGBTQI+ friendly.
